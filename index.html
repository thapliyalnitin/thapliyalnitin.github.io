Big Data Ecosystem

Introduction

The Big Data Ecosystem consists of a wide range of tools and technologies such as Hadoop, MapReduce, and BigTable designed to manage, store, and analyze large-scale datasets across multiple machines.

Computer Clusters

Computer clusters are logical units made of multiple computers (nodes) working together via a fast LAN. They are essential in processing Big Data for both scientific and business applications.

Apache Hadoop

Hadoop is an open-source Java-based framework that allows for the distributed processing of large data sets. Its ecosystem includes HDFS, MapReduce, Hive, Pig, and more.
